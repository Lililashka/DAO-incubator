Ecosystem

Decentralized autonomous organizations are a broad category of entities that live on blockchain networks. Well known DAO uses cases include mining pools and decentralized exchanges, and there are lesser known DAOs that do everything from seriousexample to funnyexample. For the most part, however, X has been out of reach/ hard to achieve. We’re interested in advancing the state of the DAO by nurturing teams who are working on implementations that are novel, socially or technologically impactful, and attainable with today’s tech.

About

DAOincubator is a pool of knowledge and experience, built for the purpose of helping DAOs become the powerful, versatile organizations we believe they can be. We study the decentralized governance space to capture insights about useful models and best practices for running organizations on the blockchain and support teams that are using this experimental technology to create organizations. We also offer consulting services related to all things DAO. Come say hi.
